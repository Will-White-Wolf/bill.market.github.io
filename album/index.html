  • ABOUT ME

    Hey, I'm Sarah. I'm half Swiss, half Guatemalan, but truly a world citizen. I grew up in six different countries and have had the privilege to see many parts of the world. If you have to ask, Albania is probably my favorite. I have over 15 years of experience documenting life through a lens. My art has been exhibited in several magazines, books, various galleries in Guatemala, as well as in the 2015 international Photography Festival, Guatephoto.

     

    I also enjoy cycling, spending time exploring the outdoors with my daughter, cooking vegan meals, reading, and traveling the world (preferably on two wheels).

     

    I love shooting dynamic events in unconventional locations. My approach to photography is one that allows maximum spontaneity and embraces each moment as it unfolds, which more often than not, isn't compatible with any kind of plans. I've found that this results in some of the most visually interesting images, and even more entertaining stories to go with them. If you're picking up what I'm putting down, I'd be thrilled to hear from you. Let's go create some everlasting magic together.
